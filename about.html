15 Q-s about life.

# Q: A:
1 How old are you? I'm in my late 30's. I was a bit younger before.
2 Are you rude? No! I'm just not political.
3 Do you have a family? Yes. Two great little fellows.
4 And any hobbies? I ride two-wheelers and I stay close to nature.
5 How do you look like? I hope that I look normally.
6 Can you speak your native language well? Yes.
7 Are you nervous? No.
8 Are you healthy? There are no healthy people. There are people that are not examined thoroughly.
9 Do you love animals? I respect life. I suspect that people who do not feel the same might have deficiencies.
10 What do you think about humour? There are no strong reasons to live without it.
11 Boys or girls? Girls.
12 What is your blood type? A Rh+ if you ever need that.
13 Tell some of the things that you would do if you were very rich? I would ride two-wheelers and stay close to nature.
14 Do you update this static site? Yes. Once or twice per year.
15 Do you know a fancy latin quote? Castigat ridendo mores.
